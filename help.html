About Us

The purpose of Forever 41 is to allow people to start and maintain a healthy lifestyle in the easiest way possible. We provide straightforward health advice tailored each individual. Being healthy should be easy and simple!

To Use

To add a health condition, go to your Dashboard and click on the +. Then, select the condition relevant to you. Then, you can view personalized advice by clicking on the specific health condition. Clicking Overview let's you view a comprehensive list of all the tips for your health conditions.
